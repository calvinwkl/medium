Small deeds are good deeds — start of my dev blog

This is my first Medium story.

We call articles here as stories. We also call 24-hour life time picutres and video posting in Facebook, Instagram or Snapchat as stories. It leads me to have a deeper thinking that the nature of stories and blogging and the idea of how we record things.

There are things that we want to record, that can also be written in papers, if not be remembered when we are grey and old, but surely not be erased in 24 hours. I am writing in here means so. I want to record those snapshots of my dev experience and hope that these will be helpful to other developers.

5 years ago (2013, writing this in 2018) I graduated with a Bachelor’s Degree in Information Engineering. Since then I started my career and have been devoted myself in the internet industry. And now I want to expand my area of skills, frontend to backend, sharing knowledge with colleagues and the community whenever it is possible to contribute to the community.

Small deeds are good deeds

All in all, I want to write down and share obstacles that I may have encountered. These things might be small and little, parts and fragments, but these will be really down to earth and all from my personal experience.

I am never a extremely skilled guy or a geek , but I would always get things done and go for a better solution whenever there is one. I sincerely hope that you will enjoy my future stories and find them useful if you are in need of them.

Happy coding!
